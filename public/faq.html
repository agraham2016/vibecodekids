Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VibeCodeKidz

Getting Started

What is VibeCodeKidz?

VibeCodeKidz is a game creation platform where kids and teens use AI assistants to build real, playable games. Just describe what you want to create, and the AI helps you build it — no coding experience needed!

What ages is it for?

VibeCodeKidz is designed for creators of all ages. We have two tiers:

  • Junior (under 13): Full COPPA protections with parental consent required. Publishing and multiplayer are parent-controlled.
  • Teen (13+): Standard safety protections with more independence. Can publish games and access community features directly.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" and choose a username, display name, and password. If you're under 13, we'll ask for your parent's email to get their permission before you can start creating.

Is it free?

Yes! We offer a free tier with 3 games per month and 10 prompts per day. Paid tiers (Creator at $13/month and Pro at $21/month) unlock more games, prompts, and premium features.

For Parents

Does my child need parental consent?

If your child is under 13, yes. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and require verifiable parental consent. You can verify via a small refundable credit card charge ($0.50) or by email confirmation.

What is the Parent Command Center?

When you approve your child's account, you'll receive a link to your Parent Command Center. From there you can:

  • Approve or deny your child's games before they go public
  • Toggle publishing and multiplayer permissions
  • Download all of your child's data
  • Delete your child's account and data
  • Revoke consent at any time

Can my child publish games publicly?

Only with your explicit approval. When your child wants to publish a game, it appears in your Parent Command Center for review. You approve or deny each game individually. You can also disable publishing entirely with a toggle.

Can my child chat with other users?

Multiplayer rooms use preset phrase-only chat (e.g., "Nice!", "Good game!"). Free-text chat is never available. Multiplayer is disabled by default for under-13 accounts and must be enabled by a parent.

What happens if my child types personal information?

Our PII (Personally Identifiable Information) scanner automatically detects and removes personal details like names, addresses, emails, phone numbers, and social media handles from prompts before they reach our AI providers. AI-generated output is also scanned before delivery.

How do I delete my child's account?

Use the Parent Command Center (linked in your consent approval email), or email admin@vibecodekidz.org. We respond within 48 hours.

Privacy & Safety

Are AI prompts shared with third parties?

AI prompts are sent to our AI providers (Anthropic and xAI) to generate game code. Before transmission, all prompts are automatically stripped of personal information. Neither provider trains their models on our data.

Does VibeCodeKidz use ads or tracking?

No. We do not display advertising, use analytics trackers (like Google Analytics), or employ social media tracking pixels. All fonts and assets are self-hosted. We do not use cookies for tracking.

Who can see my games?

Games are private by default. You choose whether to publish to the public Arcade. For under-13 users, a parent must approve each game before it goes public. Published games show only your display name (never real names or personal info).

How do I report inappropriate content?

Every published game has a "Report" button. Select a reason and our team will review it. We take reports seriously and act quickly to remove content that violates our guidelines.

Can I export or delete my data?

Yes. Teen users (13+) can export their data and delete their account from their account settings. For under-13 users, parents can do this through the Parent Command Center. You can also email us at admin@vibecodekidz.org.

Game Creation

What kinds of games can I make?

Anything your imagination can dream up! Platformers, puzzle games, racing games, RPGs, space shooters, art toys, and more. Just describe what you want and the AI will help you build it.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all! The AI writes the code for you. Just describe what you want in plain English. As you learn, you can also edit the code directly in the studio.

Can I play multiplayer games?

Yes! Games that support multiplayer let you create a room and invite friends with a 4-character code. Communication is limited to preset kid-safe phrases for everyone's safety.

What happens to games I don't publish?

They stay private in your studio. Only you can see and edit them. You can publish them later if you want, or keep them private forever.

Account & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards through Stripe. We also accept ESA (Education Savings Account) payments through ClassWallet for qualifying families.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your paid features remain active until the end of your billing period, then your account reverts to the free tier. Your games and projects are preserved.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. For under-13 users, the reset link goes to the parent email on file. For 13+ users with a recovery email, the link goes there. Otherwise, email admin@vibecodekidz.org for help.
