Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from ESA families

What is the Arizona ESA program?
The Arizona Empowerment Scholarship Account (ESA) program provides state funds to K-12 students for educational expenses including private school tuition, curriculum, online learning, tutoring, and educational technology. Families typically receive $6,000-$7,000 per year per student.
How does ClassWallet work?
ClassWallet is the digital payment platform used by the Arizona Department of Education to manage ESA funds. Parents can pay approved vendors directly through ClassWallet using their ESA balance. When you choose to pay with ClassWallet on VibeCodeKidz, you'll be redirected to confirm the purchase, and the funds are deducted from your ESA account.
Does VibeCodeKidz qualify as an approved ESA expense?
Yes. VibeCodeKidz falls under approved categories including "Curriculum," "Non-public online learning programs," and "Educational technology." Other coding platforms like CodaKid and Coco Coders are already approved ESA vendors for similar services.
How much does it cost, and which plan should I pick?
We offer two plans: Creator ($13/month or $130/year) for kids getting started with game coding, and Pro ($21/month or $210/year) for serious creators who want more games and prompts. Both are well within typical ESA allocations. Annual plans save the equivalent of 2 months free.
Is VibeCodeKidz safe for my child?
Absolutely. We are fully COPPA compliant with parental consent verification for users under 13, content filtering, and privacy protections. We never collect more data than necessary and parents can request data access or deletion at any time.
Can I use a combination of ESA funds and a credit card?
ClassWallet allows users to supplement their ESA balance with a personal credit card if needed. However, our plans are priced well within typical ESA allocations, so most families can cover the full cost with ESA funds.
When will ESA payments be available?
We are currently completing our ClassWallet vendor registration. We expect ESA payments to be live within a few weeks. Join our waitlist to be notified the moment payments go live.
