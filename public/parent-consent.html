Approve Your Child's Account

Your child has requested to create an account on VibeCodeKidz, a kid-friendly game creation platform. Under COPPA, we need your explicit consent before they can use the platform.

By approving, you consent to:
  • Collection of your child's username, display name, and age bracket
  • Storage of games your child creates on the platform
  • Use of AI services (Anthropic/xAI/OpenAI) to generate game code from your child's prompts
  • Basic anonymized usage data collection
  • If your child uses Report Bug, collection of a short bug note, up to 3 recent AI messages, a capped code excerpt, and basic technical diagnostics needed to diagnose the issue
Bug reports and support review:
  • We scan bug notes and recent chat for obvious personal information before storage
  • Automated tools may help label a bug report before a human reviews it
  • Under-13 reports receive extra review and reduced automated triage input
  • Resolved or dismissed bug reports are deleted after 90 days
Your rights as a parent:
  • Review all data collected about your child at any time
  • Toggle publishing and multiplayer features (default: OFF)
  • Request deletion of all your child's data
  • Revoke consent and deactivate the account

Want extra security? Verify with a $0.50 credit card charge (immediately refunded) for stronger identity verification.
